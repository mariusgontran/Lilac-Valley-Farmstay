Things to do

Meet Rosie The Goat

During your visit, don’t miss the chance to meet Rosie, our lovable and playful goat! She adores being fed and pampered with gentle pats, especially behind her ears. Rosie’s sweet personality and playful head nudges are sure to make your day unforgettable.

Visit the Blue Mountains

Step into the heart of Australia’s natural beauty with the majestic Blue Mountains right at our doorstep. Whether you’re planning a day hike, exploring the vibrant visitor centers, or soaking in breathtaking views at iconic viewpoints, this remarkable landscape offers unforgettable experiences for every adventurer.

Take a Dip in the Plunge Pool

After a long day of exploring, hiking, or traveling, unwind in our beatiful heater plunge pool. Allow the soothing warmth melt away your fatigue as you sink into this peaceful oasis. A complimentary bottle of wine is offered to help you enjoy the tranquil evening sky in the perfect spot to relax and recharge.
